What is Tournament of Magirus?

Tournament of Magirus is a turn-based fantasy RPG where you battle against other sorcerers with whimsical creatures along your side. You can choose to be a druid, necromancer, or fire mage in a 2D local multiplayer battle!

Roles: Producer, 2D Artist, UI Artist

Team Size: 5

Project Length: 20 weeks

Responsibilities: Schedule tasks following an agile development format, track task progress using Google sheets, organize/document playtest sessions, all 2D art, all UI art assets (except title art)

Project Status: Complete

Play our game!

Summary of Responsibilities

I delegated the team's tasks following an agile development format and kept track of them using Google Sheets and Trello.

I led bi-weekly meetings and facilitated communication through sending reminders of each members' weekly goals.

I organized playtest sessions and documented them in Google Forms.

I created all art assets such as illustrations, creature/character sprites, and UI asset art (except title art).

I concepted the designs of the creatures/characters in collaboration with our lore designer.
