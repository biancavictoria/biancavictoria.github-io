What is Momentum?

Momentum is a cyberpunk platformer where you play as Libby and kick robotic enemies with her military augmented legs. Libby joins the Resistance, who joins together to defeat and gain justice for their torn home.

Roles: Concept Artist, Sprite Artist, UI artist

Team Size: 10

Project Length: 7 months

Responsibilities: Concept cyberpunk character art and drones, create a character sprite, collaborate with other departments to ensure the concept art reflects narrative and universe

Status: Discontinued due to production

Summary of Responsibilities

I research cybernetic references and problem solved how to apply them to character concepts.

I concepted all character and enemy art and applied feedback given by the art team.

I created the main character sprite in collaboration with the character animator.

I collaborated with the design and writing teams so that concept art reflects character backstory and world.

I created dos terminal themed UI assets in collaboration with UI programmer.
