What is Continuum?

Continuum is a space fantasy roguelike where you will fight enemies to save the cursed space tower from the evil Time Lich. You encounter an interesting group of shopkeepers along the way who will help upgrade your gear!

Roles: Producer, 2D Artist, UI designer

Team Size: 7

Project Length: 20 weeks

Responsibilities: Schedule tasks following an agile development model, collaborate one on one with members to help manager their time, facilitate discussions of our task progress during meetings, concept all characters/bosses, all 2D art, design UI layouts

Status: In Progress

Play our game!

Summary of Responsibilities

I scheduled tasks following an agile development and adapt the schedule based off regularly evaluating scope.

I bettered production through meeting with members to create their personal task schedule for improved time management.

I improved communication through sending weekly task and team progress/accomplishments messages on discord.

I created all 2D art assets including illustrations, characters, icons, and animated 2D enemy sprites.

I concepted bosses and main character art for our 3D modeler to bring to life.

I designed UI layouts and create UI art assets in collaboration with our UI programmer.
